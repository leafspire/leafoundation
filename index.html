The Problem

Young people are navigating alone — and it is costing them.

As an example, Kenya has one of the youngest populations in the world. That is a staggering opportunity — and an equally staggering responsibility. Millions of young people are making defining decisions about their identities, careers, mental health, and communities without the structured support they need to make those decisions well.

The absence of mentorship is not a small gap. It compounds over time — leading to reduced confidence, poorer mental health, missed career opportunities, and communities that never benefit from the leadership sitting dormant in their youth.

The Intervention

Structured programs that meet people where they actually are.

LeafSpire does not run one-off workshops. We run cohorts — structured, recurring, relationship-based experiences that give young people time to grow, not just information to absorb. Each program is built around evidence-based principles of mentorship, peer support, and experiential learning.

Every participant enters as a learner and leaves as someone who has contributed to the community around them. Growth that only flows inward is fragile. Growth that flows outward becomes leadership.

87%
of LeafSpire alumni report improved confidence and sense of direction within 3 months of completing a program
62%
go on to take on leadership or volunteer roles — within LeafSpire or in the wider community
31
community initiatives launched by program alumni in the past 18 months alone
The Transformation

From participant to leader — that is what the data shows.

The numbers matter. But they point to something harder to quantify: the moment a young person stops seeing themselves as someone waiting to be helped and starts seeing themselves as someone capable of helping. That shift is irreversible. And it ripples outward.

Our programs do not just produce outcomes — they produce people who produce outcomes. Alumni go on to mentor others, launch initiatives, advocate in their communities, and show up for the people around them with the same intentionality that LeafSpire showed up for them.

What We Do

Five Pillars of Youth Empowerment

Our programmes are designed to develop the whole person — mind, voice, community, and future.

Mentorship & Leadership

One-on-one career guidance, peer mentorship circles, and leadership training that equip youth with confidence and purpose.

Mental Health Support

Mental health awareness campaigns, safe listening circles, and emotional resilience workshops that break stigma and foster wellbeing.

Career & Professional Development

Providing career guidance, job placement assistance, and professional development opportunities for young people.

Sustainability & SDGs

Equipping young people with climate literacy, SDG awareness, and the tools to become environmental stewards in their communities.

Social Inclusion

Ensuring no young person is left behind — creating pathways to opportunity for underserved and vulnerable youth across Kenya.
