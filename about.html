About Us

Nurturing the Leaders
The World Needs Tomorrow

"We believe that when young people are given the right support, they don't just change their own lives — they transform their communities."

— LeafSpire Founding Team

LeafSpire is a youth-centered social impact initiative rooted in Kenya and beyond. We work with young people across mentorship, mental health, community volunteering, and sustainability — giving them the tools, guidance, and spaces they need to grow into confident, responsible, and purpose-driven changemakers.

We don't just run programs — we build relationships. Every session, every conversation, every volunteer drive is a step toward a world where no young person is left behind.

Our Origin

Born From a Belief
in Young People

LeafSpire was founded in 2026 by a young leader who had experienced firsthand what happens when youth are seen, supported, and given room to grow. They built something they wished had existed for them.

What started as a small mentorship circle has grown into a multi-program initiative spanning mental health, community action, sustainability, and career development — touching lives across the continent and inspiring a global conversation about what youth empowerment truly means.

See Our Impact
Mental Wellness

A Safe Space to
Speak, Heal & Grow

We believe mental health is not a luxury — it is a foundation. Before a young person can lead, build, or dream, they need to feel safe, heard, and understood.

Our listening circles and resilience workshops create stigma-free spaces where youth can share openly and access the emotional tools they need to navigate today's world with strength and clarity.

Our Programmes
Why We Exist

Mission, Vision & Purpose

Three principles that guide everything we do at LeafSpire.

Our Mission

To empower young people with mentorship, mental health support, and community tools — so they can grow into confident, purposeful, and socially responsible leaders ready to serve their communities.

Our Vision

A world where every young person — regardless of where they come from — has the opportunity, support, and inspiration to become a positive force in their community and beyond.

Our Purpose

To bridge the gap between potential and opportunity — connecting youth with the people, programs, and platforms that unlock their ability to lead, serve, and inspire lasting change.

What We Do

How We Get Involved

Our programmes are designed to develop the whole person — mind, voice, community, and future. Explore each pillar below.

What We Offer
  • One-on-one career guidance sessions
  • Peer mentorship circles and group cohorts
  • Leadership development workshops
  • Skills training and professional readiness
Our Impact

Young people gain clarity about their futures, build confidence in their abilities, and develop the networks they need to turn ambition into action. Mentors walk alongside them — not in front.

Activities
  • Safe listening and peer support circles
  • Emotional resilience workshops
  • Mental health awareness campaigns
  • Referral pathways to professional support
Our Approach

We meet young people where they are — without judgment. Our programmes break down stigma around mental health and equip youth with practical emotional tools to navigate life's pressures with strength and clarity.

What We Organise
  • Neighbourhood clean-up and beautification drives
  • Tree planting and environmental restoration
  • School and community outreach programmes
  • Civic education and youth advocacy events
Community Involvement

Real impact starts at home. Our volunteers don't just participate — they design, lead, and own these initiatives. Each drive builds a culture of civic responsibility and collective ownership within communities.

Programme Focus
  • Climate literacy and environmental education
  • UN SDG awareness campaigns
  • Sustainable community projects and drives
  • Green entrepreneurship and innovation
Goals & Outcomes

Young people leave with a deep understanding of their role in the global sustainability agenda — and the confidence to champion it. We believe the next generation of climate leaders is already among us.

Our Commitment
  • Targeted outreach to underserved communities
  • Accessible, low-barrier programme entry
  • Inclusive design across all activities
  • Safe participation for all identities
Why It Matters

Potential is everywhere. Opportunity is not. We work to close that gap — creating pathways to growth for the youth who are most often overlooked, but have the most to offer their communities.
