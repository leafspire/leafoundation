Section 01

Introduction

Leafspire is a youth-centered social impact initiative focused on:

  • Youth empowerment and leadership development
  • Mental health and psychosocial wellbeing
  • Community service and volunteering
  • Environmental sustainability and SDG engagement
  • Inclusion and social cohesion

By accessing Leafspire programs, platforms, or donation systems, you agree to these Terms, Governance Principles, and Donor Policies.

Section 02

Non-Politicization & Neutrality Principle

🤝

Leafspire is strictly non-political, non-partisan, and non-ideological.

We explicitly state that:

  • We do not support, endorse, or affiliate with any political party, candidate, or political movement
  • We do not engage in political campaigning or election-related activities
  • Our programs are designed solely for youth development, humanitarian impact, and community wellbeing
  • Participation in Leafspire does not imply political alignment or affiliation
⚠️

Any attempt to use Leafspire's name, programs, or activities for political influence, campaigning, or propaganda is strictly prohibited.

Section 03

Startup & Evolving Structure Disclaimer

Leafspire operates as an emerging NGO initiative. This means:

  • Programs, structures, and systems are under continuous development
  • Governance frameworks may evolve as the organization grows
  • Operational limitations may exist due to funding and capacity constraints
  • We prioritize transparency, accountability, and ethical growth over commercial or political interests
Section 04

Governance Principles (UN-Style Alignment)

Leafspire is guided by the following principles:

🔍
Integrity

All actions must reflect honesty, accountability, and ethical conduct.

🌍
Inclusivity

We welcome all youth regardless of gender, background, religion, ethnicity, or socioeconomic status.

🛡️
Safeguarding

We commit to protecting vulnerable individuals, especially youth, from exploitation, abuse, or harm.

📋
Transparency

We maintain open communication regarding programs, funding use, and impact.

⚖️
Independence

Leafspire operates independently from political, corporate, or religious influence.

Section 05

Code of Conduct

All participants — volunteers, mentors, mentees, partners, and donors — must:

  • Act respectfully and responsibly
  • Avoid harassment, discrimination, or harmful behavior
  • Respect confidentiality where applicable
  • Refrain from misuse of Leafspire identity or authority
  • Uphold safeguarding standards for youth engagement
⚠️

Leafspire reserves the right to remove any participant who violates these principles.

Section 06

Volunteers, Mentees & Mentors

Volunteers
  • Act in good faith and with commitment
  • Deliver assigned responsibilities responsibly
  • Avoid misrepresentation of authority or role
  • Respect timelines and coordination structures

Volunteers are not employees unless formally stated.

Mentees
  • Participate actively in mentorship and programs
  • Respect mentors and peers
  • Engage constructively in learning spaces
  • Avoid disruptive or abusive conduct

Leafspire does not guarantee employment, academic admission, or financial outcomes.

Mentors
  • Provide guidance ethically and professionally
  • Avoid exploitation or misuse of influence
  • Respect confidentiality and boundaries
  • Support youth development in good faith
Section 07

Anti-Scam & Fraud Protection Policy

🚫

Leafspire maintains a zero-tolerance policy against fraud, impersonation, and scams.

We confirm:

  • We do not request money through personal accounts
  • Official donations are only received via verified Leafspire channels
  • We do not charge hidden "registration fees" for volunteering
  • Any suspicious activity must be reported immediately

Leafspire is not responsible for unauthorized third-party impersonation.

Section 08

Donor Policy

Nature of Donations

All donations to Leafspire are voluntary, non-compulsory, and contributed in support of youth and community programs.

Use of Funds

Donations are used for:

  • Youth mentorship programs
  • Mental health and wellbeing initiatives
  • Community outreach and environmental projects
  • Operational support for program delivery

Leafspire may allocate funds based on priority impact needs.

Transparency Commitment

  • Ethical fund management
  • Basic impact reporting where possible
  • Responsible allocation aligned with mission objectives

Donor Intent

We respect donor preferences where feasible. However, Leafspire reserves the right to redirect funds to urgent or higher-impact needs aligned with its mission.

Section 09

Refund Policy

💳

General Rule: All donations are considered final and non-refundable, except in cases of verified error.

Eligible Refund Situations

A refund may be considered only if:

  • A donation was made accidentally (duplicate or incorrect amount)
  • A technical error caused unintended transaction duplication
  • Fraudulent or unauthorized payment is proven

Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Voluntary donations made intentionally
  • Contributions already allocated to program activities
  • Donations made under misunderstanding without error evidence

Refund Request Process

To request a refund: contact Leafspire through official communication channels, provide proof of transaction. Requests will be reviewed within a reasonable timeframe. Approval is not guaranteed.

Section 10

Partners & Collaborations

Partners agree to:

  • Engage in ethical, transparent collaboration
  • Respect mutual branding agreements
  • Avoid misuse of Leafspire identity
  • Maintain non-political alignment in all joint activities

Leafspire may terminate partnerships if values or safeguarding principles are violated.

Section 11

📚

Unauthorized use, reproduction, or distribution is prohibited.

Section 12

Limitation of Liability

Leafspire is not liable for:

  • Individual decisions made by participants
  • External outcomes of participation
  • Misuse of information outside official channels
  • Actions of third parties impersonating the organization

Participation is voluntary and at personal discretion.

Section 13

Privacy & Data Protection

We handle personal data responsibly and only for:

  • Program coordination
  • Communication
  • Monitoring and evaluation
🔒

We do not sell or misuse personal data.

Section 14

Amendments

Leafspire may update these Terms periodically to reflect organizational growth and operational needs. Continued participation indicates acceptance of updated terms.
