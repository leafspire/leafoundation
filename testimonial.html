Grow & Contribute

Become a Mentee / Volunteer

Join our community of young people and volunteers. No experience required — just a desire to learn, grow, and contribute to something meaningful.

What you get as a mentee or volunteer:

  • You can join even without prior experience — all are welcome
  • Access to weekly or bi-weekly learning sessions
  • Participate online or in person — whichever works for you
  • Focus areas: career development, skills building, mentorship & leadership growth
  • A supportive community of peers and mentors invested in your journey
💚 Welcome to the LeafSpire community! We'll be in touch with your next steps.

Partner with Us

Organisations, institutions, and sponsors can collaborate with LeafSpire to amplify reach and co-create lasting change in communities.

LeafSpire partners with organisations, schools, corporates, and institutions that share our commitment to youth empowerment. Partnerships are built on shared values — not transactions.

We collaborate through programme co-design, resource sharing, joint events, and community outreach. Every partnership is unique and tailored to mutual goals.
