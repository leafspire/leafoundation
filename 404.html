Behind the Scenes

A Day as a Volunteer

What it actually looks like to show up — and what it feels like to leave knowing you made a difference today.

7:30 AM
Morning

Briefing & Intention Setting

The day starts with tea, warmth, and a circle. Volunteers share their intentions for the day — not goals, but the way they want to show up. It sets the tone for everything that follows.

9:00 AM
Workshop

Facilitating a Cohort Workshop

A room full of young people who started the morning uncertain. By the end of the session, three of them are speaking for the first time — and the room is listening. This is the work.

12:30 PM
One-on-One

One-on-One Mentorship Session

The quieter, slower, most important work. A volunteer sits across from a young person and simply listens — without agenda, without judgment. Sometimes that's the most powerful thing you can offer.

4:00 PM
Close of Day

Team Debrief & Community Moment

The day ends the way it started — in a circle. Volunteers share what surprised them, what moved them, what they'll carry home. Nobody leaves unchanged. That's not a coincidence. It's the design.

Your Volunteer Journey

From application to leadership — here is how it works.

1

Apply

Tell us who you are and what moves you. No experience required — just genuine heart.

2

Connect

Meet the team. We match you with the programme that fits your strengths and schedule.

3

Orientation

A full onboarding day where you learn our values, methods, and meet your cohort.

4

Participate

Show up. Facilitate. Listen. Support young people as they find their voices and path.

5

Lead

The best volunteers become mentors to the next wave. The cycle of impact continues.
